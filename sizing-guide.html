Fit Tips

  • Seat height: child should be able to put one foot flat on the ground while seated.
  • Reach: child should comfortably reach the handlebars with a slight bend in the elbows.
  • Balance bikes (Woom 1) are best to learn steering and balance before pedals.
  • If between sizes, choose the smaller size for control or the larger for growth depending on your child’s skill.
