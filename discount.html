How the $25 Woom Discount Works

This page explains how to activate your $25 Woom discount and what to do if the savings don’t apply at checkout.

Activate $25 Discount

If the discount doesn’t appear, try disabling ad-blocking or open the link in a private window.

Step-by-step

  1. Click the Activate button above and land on the Woom site.

  3. Verify the $25 deduction is shown before payment.

Troubleshooting

Ad-blockers, tracker-blockers, or aggressive privacy settings commonly prevent referral/discount tags. Disable extensions or try another browser if needed.
